ACCESSIBILITY

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock accessibility_content, type:text %}

At The Frederick Hotel we are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, which is why we are working diligently to ensure it meets ADA standards, including a level AA conformance to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0. We expect to meet these standards by December 2017.

While we understand that the current site has its limitations, be assured that we are working to fix them as soon as possible. That said, if you have have come to this page because of a problem you encountered on the site, please do not hesitate in reporting it to us at:

  • The Frederick Hotel
  • 95 West Broadway,
  • New York, NY 10007
  • Phone - 212.566.1900
  • Email - info@triumphny.com

In addition, if you have any suggestions on how to improve this site's accessibility, we are listening. Your user experience matters to us, and we want to ensure it is as good as it can be.

This accessibility statement applies to all content on www.frederickhotelnyc.com and all content provided internally at www.frederickhotelnyc.com . The Frederick Hotel is not responsible for any third-party content on www.frederickhotelnyc.com .
